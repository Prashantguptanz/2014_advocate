AdvoCate - Adventures of Categories - provides a computational framework to explore and conceptualize changes in concepts and categories, as changes happen in other aspects of the scientific investigation, for example, new observations, or change in classification method. AdvoCate allows to study and understand changes in concepts and categories, or evolution in scientific knowledge in breadth and depth. Changes are studied at different facets - intension, computational intension, extension and relationships. Changes in concepts and categories are captured as they evolve with time and also conflicting changes that may happen due to different perspectives of communities/domains. We further study change epistemology by capturing the whole life cycle of change, starting from various internal and external change triggers, then conceptualization and validation of changes, then applying changes through change operations and realizing their effects through the structural and semantic changes to concepts and categories, and finally the distribution of those changes to the related artifacts.
